## A B S T R A C T

This paper presents a comprehensive framework for implementing digital twin
technology in heritage building conservation. The proposed methodology integrates
Building Information Modeling (BIM) with real-time sensor data to create a
dynamic digital representation of historic structures.

## 1. I n t r o d u c t i o n

Heritage buildings represent an important part of cultural identity and require
careful conservation strategies (Smith et al., 2020). Traditional inspection
methods are often time-consuming and may not capture the full extent of
deterioration patterns (Jones & Brown, 2021).
